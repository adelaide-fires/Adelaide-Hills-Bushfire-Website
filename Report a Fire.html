Triple Zero

When to call:

  • If someone is seriously injured or requires urgent medical attention
  • If your life or property is being threatened
  • If you've just witnessed a serious accident or crime

How to call:

  • Call 000 from a safe location
  • Clearly specify if you need Police, Fire or Ambulance
  • Provide your town and exact location
  • When connected, answer the operator's questions and don't hang up until instructed


For more information, visit CFS: Triple Zero Information
